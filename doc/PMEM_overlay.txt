VSPA PMEM OVERLAY:
******************
- The current vspa image has 2 overlay PMEM sections.
- VSPA PMEM section name, where the overlay gets downloaded to, is ".vpram_overlay" 
- PMEM section names, that the ARM needs to download from, are ".IQ_data_ovl_ddr" and ".CAL_ovl_ddr"

Overlay sections:

1- IQ_data_ovl:
   - This overlay section has the opcodes related to the IQ data transfer 
   - The name of the section that ARM needs to download into vspa is ".IQ_data_ovl_ddr"
   - The opcodes included in this section:
	a- opcode 1 (TX single tone): generate a single tone and transmit it to the AXIQ
	b- opcode 2 (RX single tone measurement): receive a tone from the AXIQ and calculate the SNR
	c- opcode 5 (TX IQ samples): transmit IQ data from DDR to the AXIQ
	d- opcode 6 (RX IQ samples): receive IQ data from the AXIQ to DDR

2- CAL_ovl:     
   - This overlay section has the opcodes related to the calibration procedure
   - The name of the section that ARM needs to download into vspa is ".CAL_ovl_ddr"
   - The opcodes included in this section:
	c- opcode 3 (DC offset calibration): TX and RX DC offset
	d- opcode 4 (RC calibration): BW calibration
